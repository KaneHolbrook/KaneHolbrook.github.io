Glossary of Terms

Acetic Acid -Contributes greatly to the percieved acid taste of coffee. Has been implicated with a sour taste.

Alanine- A nonessential amino acid. Found in a variety of foods.

Amino Acids- An organic compound that has an acid functional group and an amine functional group on adjacent carbon atoms. The building blocks of proteins and the basic constituents of life.

Arabica - A species of coffee indigenous to Ethiopia. Has a higher sugar content than Robusta.

Asparagine- A nonessential amino acid. Reacts with reducing sugars to produce acrylamide.
